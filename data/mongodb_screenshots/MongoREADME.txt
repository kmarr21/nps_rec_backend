Each of the screenshots is named after one of the collections in the MongoDB database (NPS). 
Each screenshot is an example of one of the records and the fields available in that record.

Collections:
parksAllData - master collection with parks, monthly_visitation and weather
parksWithVisitaion - collection with parks and monthly_visitation
parks - just parks data pulled from NPS API
weather_nps - weather per park (temp, humidity, precipitation)
monthly_visitation - visitation per park
